Zayn Malik

Zayn Malik (born January 12, 1993, in Bradford, England) is a British singer and songwriter who first became famous as a member of the boy band One Direction. He joined the group in 2010 after joining The X Factor UK as a solo contestant, and soon won hearts around the world with his charming looks and soulful voice. Together with One Direction, he released hit songs like What Makes You Beautiful and Story of My Life. In 2015, Zayn decided to leave the group to focus on his solo career. His first album, Mind of Mine, featured the popular song Pillowtalk, showing his smooth R&B style. Fans love Zayn for his deep voice, confident attitude, and creative personality that make him stand out in the music world.
